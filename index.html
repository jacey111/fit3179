Global Medication Usage for Anxiety and Depression in 2020

Mental health can affect a person's day-to-day life, relationships, and physical health. External factors in people's lives and relationships can also contribute to their mental well-being. In 2020, the number of people living with anxiety and depressive disorders rose significantly because of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Age at First Experience of Anxiety or Depression

The most common age group for the first experience of anxiety or depression is between 20-29 years old. Regionally, this trend is particularly evident in lower-middle-income countries and Europe, where younger onset is more prevalent. Conversely, in high-income countries and upper-middle-income countries, older age groups (30 and above) show a higher occurrence.
