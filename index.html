Mental Health Insights

Mental health can affect a person's day-to-day life, relationships, and physical health. External factors in people's lives and relationships can also contribute to their mental well-being. In 2020, the number of people living with anxiety and depressive disorders rose significantly because of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Mental health disorders affect individuals across all ages, but the age at which these challenges first manifest can vary widely based on geographical, socio-economic, and cultural factors. Early identification of anxiety or depression is crucial, as they can significantly impact personal development, educational attainment, and overall quality of life.
